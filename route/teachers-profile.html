Research Grants

Our eminent faculty members have been granted several research SERB, DST and other government and prominent non-government funded projects in regular intervals which produced innovative and productive research works in the field of Technology and Scientific developments. Listed projects are only few of them.

• Dr. Sourav Bhattacharya, Biotechnology Department, "High frequency in-vitro propagation of Porteresia coarctata and analysis of polyamine content under salinity stress”, SERB, 19.34 lakhs, 2019.

• Prof. Harendra Nath Bhattacharya, Earth Science Department, SERB, DST Sponsored project “A comprehensive sedimentological model for accreting tidal flats in a modern wave-dominated Subarnarekha river mouth estuarine system along the coast of Bay of Bengal, eastern India, SERB, DST, 5.17 lakhs, 2019.

• Dr. Srabani Karmakar, Biotechnology Department, “Molecular mechanism of the protein aggregation and its modulation by peptide chaperones in myopathy caused by mutations in αB crystallin” , SERB (TARE), 18.30 Lakhs, 2018.

• Ms. Amrita Muherjee, Earth Science Department, Women Scientist Project, DST “Sedimentation pattern in a paleoproterzoic continental rift basin, aravalli supergroup of Udaipur Valley, Rajasthan”, DST, Govt. of India, 15.81 lakhs, 2018.

• Dr. Mourani Sinha, Mathematics Department, “Estimation of design and operational wave height statistics to assess coastal vulnerability along the east coast of India”, Space Application Centre (ISRO), Ahmedabad, 16.66 lakhs, 2017.

• Dr. Mourani Sinha, Mathematics Department, “Data Adaptive Ocean Parameters from SCATSAT-1 Wind”, ISRO, Ahmedabad, 32.08 lakhs, 2017.

• Dr. Atanu Kotal, Chemistry Department, “Synthesis of Sequence-Regulated Functional Polymers by Controlled/Living Radical Polymerization and its Application towards Material Science”, SERB, 17 lakhs, 2016

• Prof. Asit Baran Bhattacharya, Electronics and Communication Engineering Department, “Signal degradation at VLF to VHF bands due to transient disturbances”, Radio Astronomy Supplies, Texas, USA and NASA supported observatories of USA , INR 92 lakhs, 2016.

• Dr. Mourani Sinha, Mathematics Department, “Telecommunications of ENSO and IOD on Ocean Wave Parameters”, SERB under DST, 14.99 Lakhs, 2014.
